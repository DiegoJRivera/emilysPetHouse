About Us:


Emily's Pet House was born to meet the needs of both people and pets. Our services are focused on those who consider their pets as a member of their family, those who understand and consistently live daily the responsibility of having a pet. That's why at Emily's Pet House we focus so much on the health, integrity and style of our animals.


We are interested in transmitting the love and respect for the animals and we see them as members of our families, that is why we are looking for the owners to be aware of their environment, the environment in which they act. Our services are oriented to all those who look for a place that allows the pets a correct interaction with the environment and the other way around.
